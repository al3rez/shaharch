Why Shaharch?

Voice notes take 3 minutes to type.

We transcribe them in 2 seconds. Press Super+T. Speak. Done.

OpenAI-powered transcription directly to your clipboard.

You waste 30 seconds finding the right theme.

We switch everything in 1 keypress. Super+Shift+Ctrl+Space. Instant beauty.

6 curated themes. Terminal, browser, editor - all change together.

Terminal lag ruins your flow.

Foot terminal: 0ms startup. Native Wayland. Zero bloat.

While others load, you're already working.

Notifications interrupt deep work.

Super+Ctrl+, = Focus mode. One key. Total silence.

Smart notification management that respects your time.

Your eyes hurt after sunset.

Hyprsunset runs automatically. Blue light gone. Eyes happy.

No configuration needed. It just works.

Privacy-invading browsers track everything.

Zen Browser by default. Your data stays yours.

Fast, secure, and respects your privacy out of the box.

68 keybindings. 6 themes. 1 command to install.

Everything configured. Nothing to set up.
